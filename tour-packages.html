Nakupenda Sandbank

Embark on a day trip to Nakupenda Island, a tranquil paradise just 3.5 miles from Stone Town, Zanzibar. Explore the stunning sandbank named Nakupenda, meaning "I Love You" in Swahili.

Indulge in snorkeling, sunbathing, and a feast of seafood and seasonal fruits. Immerse yourself in the natural reserve's charm, relax on white sands, and discover marine life before sailing back to Stone Town. Nakupenda, a coastal gem in Zanzibar, invites you to a day of pure bliss.

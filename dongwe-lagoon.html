Dongwe Lagoon

Explore the Vibrant Underwater World of Zanzibar's Blue Lagoon

  • Introduction
  • REVIEW
  • Map

Enjoy an amazing snorkeling experience in the Best Lagoon of the East Coast of Zanzibar! Visit Dongwe Beach where you will onboard in your sailing outriggers on the way to Blue Lagoon. Swim among hundreds of fishes, and explore colorful starfish, nice corals and some sea lions.

Once you have the snorkeling gear and feel comfortable you will make your way out to Blue Lagoon Atoll on a motorized boat. Your first snorkeling location will be guided through deep waters, home of huge schools of fish and wonderful coral reefs.

Next you will go to a shallower location where the marine life and vegetation live closer to the surface and you will enjoy observing from closer range.

You will also spend your own time at the sandbank before driving back to mark the end of your adventure.
